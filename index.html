Frequently Asked Questions

Does a small business or medium enterprise (SME) also need an audit?
Yes, depending on turnover and company type. SMEs with annual turnover above Rs. 10 million or private limited companies are generally required to have their accounts audited under SECP regulations.
How often should I conduct an audit for my business in Pakistan?
Annual audits are mandatory for most registered companies. However, internal audits can be conducted quarterly or bi-annually for better financial control and risk management.
How long does the audit process usually take?
Typically 2-4 weeks depending on company size and complexity. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your business operations while ensuring thorough examination.

Registration FAQs

How long does registration take?
Typically 7-15 working days depending on registration type. SECP company registration usually takes 10-15 days, while partnership/AOP registration can be completed in 5-7 days.
What type of company should I choose?
It depends on your business needs. For solo ventures, consider SMC or sole proprietorship. For multiple partners, choose partnership/AOP or LLP. For growth and investment, Private Limited Company is ideal.
Is SECP registration mandatory?
For companies (Pvt Ltd, SMC, LLP), yes. For sole proprietorship and partnerships, SECP registration is optional but recommended for credibility and legal protection.

Tax Filing FAQs

How long does the tax filing process take?
Typically 3-7 working days once all required documents are provided. Complex cases may take up to 10 days.
Is income tax return filing mandatory for salaried individuals?
Yes, if your annual income exceeds Rs. 600,000 or if tax was deducted from your salary, filing is mandatory to claim refunds and maintain ATL status.
What if I missed the deadline for income tax filing?
You can still file with late filing penalties. We help minimize penalties and ensure proper filing even after deadlines.
Can I file taxes without an NTN?
No, NTN is mandatory for tax filing. We can help you obtain your NTN quickly through the FBR IRIS system.
